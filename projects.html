Squarefold

Furniture of the future

For the 2019 Entrepreneurship challenge for the engineering students in Dublin High, alongside my team, I designed a versatile and modular furniture system that utilized ground-pistons in order to raise and lower tables and chairs to compensate for the decreasing amount of space in most cities. Coupled with the tiled ground, we also developed a program that can be used by the house or building owners and it can allow for premade room setups and more. Our team demonstrated our model and made it to the presentational rounds after winning 1st in our Principles of Engineering class.
